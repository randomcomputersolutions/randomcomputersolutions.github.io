Refund & cancellation policy

Last updated: 5 July 2026

Digital delivery

Activation codes are digital products delivered instantly after payment. They are generated specifically for the serial number you submit and cannot be reused, resold, or returned.

No refund after delivery

Once an activation code has been generated and displayed after a successful payment, the purchase is final and non-refundable.

When we do refund

You are entitled to a refund in these cases:

  • The amount was deducted from your account but no activation code was delivered.
  • You were charged more than once for the same serial number (duplicate payment).

Contact us within 7 days of the payment at sanvanbafna@gmail.com with your user ID and the payment reference. Verified refunds are processed to the original payment method within 5–7 business days.

Cancellation

Because delivery is instant, an order cannot be cancelled after payment is completed. If a payment is pending or fails, no code is issued and any deducted amount is automatically reversed by the payment provider.
