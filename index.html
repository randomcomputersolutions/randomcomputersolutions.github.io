About us

Random Computer Solutions develops and supports Random XP, a billing and accounting software used by our registered customers. Each installation of Random XP is licensed per computer and is unlocked using an activation code.

Our service — software activation codes

Registered Random XP customers purchase activation codes online through our Android application:

  • Enter your customer user ID and the serial number shown by Random XP.
  • Pay securely online (UPI, cards, netbanking) — payments are processed by Razorpay.
  • Your activation code is generated and shown instantly after successful payment.
  • You can re-view the code for your current serial number any time, free of charge.

Get the app

Payments are collected exclusively through our Android application, where registered customers enter their customer user ID and pay securely via Razorpay Checkout. There is no payment or login on this website.
