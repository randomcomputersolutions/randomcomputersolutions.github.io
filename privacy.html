Privacy policy

Last updated: 5 July 2026

This privacy policy explains how Random Computer Solutions ("we", "us") collects and uses information when you use our Android application and services to purchase software activation codes.

Information we collect

  • Customer details: your customer user ID, name, and contact details that we hold as part of your existing customer relationship with us.
  • Activation data: the serial numbers you submit, the activation codes issued to you, the date of activation, and the amount charged.
  • Payment information: payments are processed entirely by Razorpay Software Pvt. Ltd. We never receive or store your card number, UPI PIN, or banking credentials. We receive only a payment reference (order ID and payment ID) to confirm your payment.

How we use this information

  • To verify that you are a registered customer eligible to receive activation codes.
  • To generate and deliver your activation code after successful payment.
  • To maintain a history of your activations so you can re-view your current code free of charge.
  • To respond to support and refund requests.

Sharing of information

We do not sell or rent your personal information. Data is shared only with the service providers required to run this service:

  • Razorpay — to process your payment (see the Razorpay privacy policy at razorpay.com).
  • Supabase — our secure database and server hosting provider, where customer and activation records are stored.

Data retention and security

Records are kept for as long as you remain our customer and as required for accounting purposes. Access to our systems is restricted and protected by authentication. All communication between the app and our servers is encrypted (HTTPS).

Your rights
